Refund & Return Policy

At Sajji Junction, quality is our priority. Since we serve perishable food items, we do not accept returns once the food has been accepted by the customer. However, if there is a quality issue or an incorrect order, please contact us within 30 minutes of delivery for a replacement or a credit note for your next order.

Delivery Policy

We deliver within our specified zones in Lahore and Karachi. Estimated times are usually 45-60 minutes depending on woodfire roasting time. Large platters may require extra prep time. Minimum order values apply for free delivery.
